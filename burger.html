Travis Scott Burger 2: Electric Boogaloo

Bring ASTROWORLD right into your kitchen with this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Brioche Hamburger bun
  • 2 strips of thick cut bacon
  • 1/4 lb ground beef
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 4-5 pickles
  • 2 slices of American cheese
  • 1 head of lettuce, shredded

Instructions

  1. Drop the bacon onto a cold pan, then bring the heat to a medium. Cook until crispy then remove and place on top of a napkin
  2. Shape the ground beef into a hamburger patty, and season with salt and pepper
  3. Heat up a skillet on high heat with butter. Toast the hamburger buns until they're golden brown then remove and set aside
  4. Adding more butter to the pan, drop the patty and continue to flip until it's crispy. Add your cheese and cover the pan to let the cheese melt. Once melted, take the burger off the heat
  5. Starting with the bottom bun, add the patty, bacon, lettuce, pickles, then onion
  6. Cover the top bun with ketchup and mayo, then top the burger.
